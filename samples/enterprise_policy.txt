Enterprise AI Approval Policy

Critical business recommendations require manager approval before distribution.
Critical actions include revenue forecasts, staffing recommendations, financial risk alerts, customer churn interventions, and automated vendor communications.

The AI system must generate a report with citations, confidence indicators, known limitations, and recommended next steps.
If the workflow detects anomalies, data quality issues, model accuracy below threshold, or missing source context, it must notify the operations manager.

Approved workflows may send final reports to stakeholders. Rejected workflows must be marked for human review.
