Customer Feedback Summary

Several enterprise customers reported slower response times during April and May. The most common concern was delayed inventory availability for high-priority orders.
Positive feedback increased after support teams began proactive status updates.

Key themes:
- Customers want earlier warning when inventory is constrained.
- Satisfaction drops sharply when delivery delays exceed seven days.
- Renewal risk is highest for accounts with repeated support escalations.
- Customers responded well to personalized remediation offers.
